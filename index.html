Meet Dr Maphakela

Dr Molefi L. Maphakela is a healthcare practitioner specialising as a General Practitioner (GP) at the Centre of Advanced Medicine in Waverley, Johannesburg.

He also serves as the School Doctor at St John’s College in Houghton and as the Team Doctor for the Orlando Pirates Football Club, bringing extensive experience in adolescent and sports medicine to his everyday practice.

His approach is calm, attentive and evidence-based, with an emphasis on prevention, early diagnosis and clear explanations so that patients feel informed and involved in their care.

Clinical Focus Areas

  • General & family medicine
  • Chronic disease management (e.g. diabetes, hypertension)
  • Sports, school & adolescent health
  • Acute illnesses & infections
  • Minor procedures & wound care
  • Preventative health & screenings

How we can help you

A full-service general practice tailored to the needs of busy individuals, families, learners and athletes.

General & Family Consultations

Comprehensive care for everyday concerns – from colds and flu to chronic conditions and follow-ups.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing support for hypertension, diabetes, asthma and other chronic illnesses with regular monitoring.

Preventative Care & Screening

Health checks, blood tests and screening tailored to your age, lifestyle and risk factors.

Sports & School Health

Medicals for school and sport, injury assessment and return-to-play guidance for active patients.

Women’s & Men’s Health

Discreet consultations covering reproductive health, hormonal concerns and wellness checks.

Minor Procedures

Procedures such as wound suturing, abscess drainage, injections and other in-room treatments.
